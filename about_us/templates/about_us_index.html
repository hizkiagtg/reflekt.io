About Us

Mental health journal for your healthy living during the pandemic

Mental health is one of the most important things to pay attention to amid the COVID-19 pandemic. We believe that life is getting harder by the day after more than a year of living under the coronavirus threat with limited social interaction. Many people suffer from depression and frustration because they no longer have a place to confide in due to the awkwardness of interacting with other people. Therefore, we created a website application that can be a new safe place for everyone named reflekt.io. This website application has various features which hopefully can help us maintain our mental health during this hard situation. We hope that this web application will allow people to live life more easily during this pandemic.

Our Features

Journaling

A private place for users to express their thoughts and emotions throughout the day.

Ide Kegiatan

Activity recommendations based on users' journaling results to improve their mental health.

Pojok Curhat

A public place for users to confide all of the thoughts and emotions that are haunting them.

Tembok Harapan

A private place for users to write down all the wishes that they want to achieve.

Kutipan Penyemangat

A place for users to submit their inspirational quotes, which will be published publicly.

Deteksi Dini Depresi

A tool that helps users to find out if they are currently suffering from depression or not.
